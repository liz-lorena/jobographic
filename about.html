The Site

This website uses the Google Maps Embed API, the Glassdoor API, the Github Jobs API, and the Google Places Autocomplete API for a responsive and accurate interactive map and job-searching experience that allows users to find jobs and companies based on their location and keywords and to see the path and travel time between the user and the job or company that interests them. This project took us over a week to create as a final project for our Fall 2017 Advanced Web Design Course, and after much hard work and finding ways to work around our limitations, we bring to you with much pride Jobographic.
